    Summary

    The goal of this project is to create a dashboard of the current US Senate, in order to display the performance of the senators. The purpose of the project is to provide necessary information to voters in order to empower them to make informed choices.

    Introduction:

    Who’s your Senator? The year 2020 will be one for the history books! All over the world, there is civil unrest; all politically charged. In the United States, the political climate has its citizens enthralled in the political process now more than ever leaving it’s people to pay closer attention to those who help to make the laws that affect everyone’s daily lives. A good place to start would be with your senators. Senators come from all walks of life and they are tasked with the job of promoting and passing legislation, utilizing a collaborative approach to problem solving, having a competitive temperament, and the ability to juggle the interests of its tax payers, local business leaders, and other government officials. Explore our database of senators and find out who is your direct link to Congress!
